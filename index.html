Hannah and Ivan's Wedding

Thank you for visiting our wedding website! Please see some practical information below. We look forward to seeing you on Saturday 6 August!

Schedule

11.10-11.30 Guests arrive

11.30-c13.00 Wedding ceremony

c13.00-14.00 Cloisters drinks receptions

14.00-17.30 Break (tours, pub lunches, baby naps etc)

17.30 Guests reconvene at the Bodleian Library for drinks in the Old Schools Quadrangle (Proscholium in case of rain)

18.15 Guests are called to dinner

20.30-22.30 Music and dancing

22.30-23.00 Guests depart
